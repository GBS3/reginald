Reginald

Meet your new assistant

Reginald is an application for Windows 10 designed to make your life easier and more productive.

Download Reginald

Application launcher

Reginald can help you find your applications quickly and effortlessly. To get started, open the search box by pressing Alt + Space.

Web Searches

Quickly launch websites and make web searches without the hassle of opening a new tab by using some of Reginald's built-in keywords.

Light & Dark Modes

Feel more productive with a lighter background? Simply go into Reginald's settings and turn off dark mode to enable light mode.
